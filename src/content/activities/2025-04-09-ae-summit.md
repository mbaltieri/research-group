The “AE: Global Summit on Open Problems for AI” was held in Tokyo,
April 9th-11th 2025, at Sakura Deeptech Shibuya in Shibuya. 
The summit brought together leading global experts to engage in discussions 
on the development of AI algorithms, ethics, and social implementation.

Kai Arulkumaran took the stage on Day 1 – Algorithms Day (April 9th) as a 
keynote speaker. 
He gave a presentation titled “Neuro-inspired AI: Reinforcement Learning and 
Consciousness” and participated in the panel discussion **Is Scale Enough?**.

![Kai](ae-araya.jpg)

At the kickoff of the event, Araya was featured alongside Sakana AI, RIKEN, 
and Rakuten in the context of Japan’s historical contributions to AI as well 
as its current landscape of entrepreneurship.
