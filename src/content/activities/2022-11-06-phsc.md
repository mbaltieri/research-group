Hiro Hamada speaks in Session 01 at Keio University (Mita Campus).

Hiro Hamada will talk at The Japanese Association for Philosophy 
of Science 2022 Fall Regular Meeting in session 01 starting at 
9:45 am.
The presentation’s theme will be “Considering the Public Role of 
Artificial Entities Learning from Human Data”.
The Japanese Association for Philosophy of Science was founded in 
February 1954 to promote research studies focusing on the foundations 
of science, to establish and maintain contacts with researchers in 
foreign countries, and to contribute to the development of the 
philosophy of science.
