Masakazu Inoue presented a poster at the OHBM Annual Meeting, titled
**Transient cortical activity predicts spontaneous attention shifts**.

The Organization for Human Brain Mapping (OHBM) Annual Meeting is one 
of the largest gatherings in the field of neuroimaging.
It was held in Seoul, Korea, 26th-27th June 2024. 

The OHBM Annual Meeting is a large-scale event where thousands of 
presentations are made every year, and it serves as a hub for the latest 
research in neuroimaging.
