On Thursday, July 17, 2025, Manuel Baltieri, Sarah Cosentino and Marina 
Di Vincenzo joined as neurotechnology experts at a round table discussion 
“Brain Resilience in a Changing World: A Conversation Between Italian 
and Japanese Scientists” at Expo 2025 Osaka.

![Araya Team at Expo 2025](expo-araya.jpg)

The event brought together top researchers, policy makers, and leaders 
from Italy and Japan to discuss how neuroscience can enhance the mental 
resilience of our minds in an increasingly complex world. 
Held at the Italy Pavilion, the event bridged Italian and Japanese 
insights on mental resilience.
