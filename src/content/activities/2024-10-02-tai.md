On March 13th, 2025, Kai Arulkumaran co-hosted “TAI AAI #04 – Brain-inspired AI.”

At Tokyo AI (TAI), they will host several researchers from academia and 
industry to present their work on brain-inspired AI, with topics ranging 
from AI agents implementing theories of consciousness to developmental 
robotics. 
Rousslan Dossa was also invited to speak on the day.
