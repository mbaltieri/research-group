Manuel Baltieri and Sarah Cosentino gave a presentation at Breakthrough 
Discuss 2025 held at the University of Oxford in the UK, April 23rd-24th 
2025.

Breakthrough Discuss is an international conference held annually on the 
theme of extraterrestrial life and the search for intelligent life in 
the universe. 
This year’s theme is “Life As We Don’t Yet Know It”.

Manuel's presentation, titled “Life and its relatives”, was part of 
Session 1: **Forms of non-terrestrial Life** 

![Manuel](breakthrough-manuel.jpg)

Sarah's presentation, titled “Reflecting Emotion: The Path to Robot 
Consciousness”, was featured in Session 2: **The nature of consciousness
and intelligence**.
