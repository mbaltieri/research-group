The goal of this workshop is to bring together information theorists and neuroscientists to discuss the relations between information-theoretic concepts and the foundations of agency.

Organised by Araya team members (and former team members):
- Ryota Kanai (Araya Inc.)
- Masafumi Oizumi (The University of Tokyo)
