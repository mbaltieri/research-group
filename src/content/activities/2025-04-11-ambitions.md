An interview with Hiro Hamada, entitled “分散を社会実装できるか：
神経科学から辺境まで (Japanese Only)” has been published in the 
book “攻殻機動隊　MESSED　MESH　AMBITIONS.”
The article discusses a wide range of topics, from the history 
and ideas of Web3 that led to DeSci (decentralized science) to 
Hiro's ambitions for social implementation. 
The book was released by KODANSHA Ltd. on April 11, 2025.
