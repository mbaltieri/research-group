Shuntaro Sasai was invited to be a panelist at the JBMIS2024 
Symposium, held on October 12th, 2024.
This symposium brought together experts in R&D from academia and 
industry to discuss the industrialization of BMIs. 
It focused on their societal implementation in medicine and other 
fields based on cutting-edge neuroscience and medical engineering research.
