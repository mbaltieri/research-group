Dr. Kai Arulkumaran, Team Leader of the Reinforcement Learning Team in the Research and Development Department at Araya Inc., participated as a panelist and a presenter at IEEE RO-MAN 2025 - "3rd Workshop on NeuroDesign in Human-Robot Interaction” on Monday, August 25th, 2025.  

During the event, the world’s leading experts discussed how to use neuroscience and behavioral psychology to build human robot interactions (HRI) into practical consumer products, as well as addressing the current challenges of that.
